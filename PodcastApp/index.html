OUR APP

We didn't build an app, we built a community. Podcasting has never had a centralized place to discuss the shows we all love and listen to. We've brought a great podcast listening experience together with a world-class community section so you can chat, share, compare and analyze every aspect of every minute of every show. We love it, and we hope you do too. Try Podcast App today.

ABOUT PODCAST APP

Podcast app is the brainchild of Seth Archer, that's me. After trying to share my favorite moments from podcasts on social media platforms, I became intensely frustrated. It doesn't have to be hard, I just wanted to talk about my favorite shows with people. I realized that even when I managed to post a moment, or a link to traditional social media, my friends didn't care. They don't listen to the shows I do, and they may not even be 'podcast people.' I made Podcast App to create a community around the people that love podcasting, and embrace it as a quirky medium.

Right now, podcasts are an intensely personal medium. Podcast App changes that. It brings your feelings about the hosts and the topics to the forefront of the conversation, and lets you share that with people.
